About Us

We are a group of friends based in Ann Arbor, MI who share a passion for golf. Like many, we juggle demanding jobs, family commitments, and everything in between. That’s why we created Casual Golf Guys – a space for golfers in the greater Ann Arbor area to connect, find playing partners, and build lasting friendships.

In the Spring of 2024, we formed Casual Golf Guys to support and grow the Ann Arbor Casual Golf Group (AACGG), a local community focused on fostering meaningful connections on the course. Through AACGG, golfers of all levels can participate in friendly rounds, meet new people, and expand their networks – all while keeping things casual!

Since the group’s launch, AACGG has organized two fantastic events:

  • The First Annual AACGG Summer Invitational
  • The First Annual AACGG Fall Classic Scramble

We’re excited about the future and look forward to supporting more events, welcoming new members, and continuing to help create unforgettable golf moments for the Ann Arbor community!
