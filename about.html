About Us

We are a group of friends based in Ann Arbor, MI who share a passion for golf. Like many, we juggle demanding jobs, family commitments, and everything in between. That’s why we created Casual Golf Guys – a space for golfers in the greater Ann Arbor area to connect, find playing partners, and build lasting friendships.

Founded in the spring of 2024, our mission is simple: foster meaningful connections on the course that lead to great golf memories and even better networking opportunities – all while keeping things casual!

Since launching, we’ve hosted two incredible events:

  • The First Annual AACGG Summer Invitational
  • The First Annual AACGG Fall Classic Scramble

We’re excited about the future and look forward to adding more events, welcoming new members, and continuing to create unforgettable golf moments together!
